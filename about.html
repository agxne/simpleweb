About Ozamiz City

History

Ozamiz was named after Senator José Ozámiz, a hero of World War II. It is historically known for Fort Santiago (Cotta Shrine), built during the Spanish period. The city later became a cultural hub and trade center of Misamis Occidental.

Festival

The Subayan Keg Subanon Festival, now known as the Perangat Festival, celebrates the culture of the Subanen people. The festival features traditional music, colorful dances, and rituals.
